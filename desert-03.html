Chocolate brownie and ice cream

Serving: 1 person

Price: R45.99

  • Chocolate brownie is made from flour, cocoa, sugar, eggs, vanilla essence, butter and a pinch of salt. The ingredients are mixed well to form the brownice batter.
  • The batter is put into a flat square oven dish, and then put into the oven.
  • Once baked the brownie is cut up into equal square pieces
  • 1 large scoop of ice cream is placed in the middle of the waffle
  • Chocolate shavings are sprinkled on the brownie and ice cream.
